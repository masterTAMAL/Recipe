Chocolate Cake
Prep: 45 mins Servings: 4
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir well.
  4. Pour into baking tin.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.
Margherita Pizza
Prep: 20 mins Servings: 2
  • 250g pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add mozzarella and basil leaves.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
Pasta Alfredo
Prep: 25 mins Servings: 3
  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add cream.
  3. Stir in parmesan cheese.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Toss pasta in sauce and serve.
Grilled Chicken Salad
Prep: 15 mins Servings: 2
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup lettuce leaves
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup cucumber slices
  • Salad dressing
  1. Grill chicken until cooked.
  2. Chop into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Add lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber to a bowl.
  4. Place chicken on top.
  5. Drizzle dressing before serving.
Blueberry Pancakes
Prep: 20 mins Servings: 3
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder.
  2. Add milk and stir until smooth.
  3. Fold in blueberries gently.
  4. Pour batter onto hot pan.
  5. Flip when bubbles form, cook until golden.
